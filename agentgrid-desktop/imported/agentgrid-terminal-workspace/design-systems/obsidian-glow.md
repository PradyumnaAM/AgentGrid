## Brand & Style

The design system is engineered for professional developers who demand a high-performance environment that balances raw technical power with a premium, focused aesthetic.

## Layout & Spacing

Fluid Grid optimized for widescreen monitors. Gutters are fixed at 24px. All spacing should be multiples of the 4px base unit.

## Components

Primary buttons use amber background with black text. Secondary buttons use transparent background with bronze border. Inputs use dark charcoal background and amber focus. Cards use glassmorphic gradients and subtle inner strokes.
